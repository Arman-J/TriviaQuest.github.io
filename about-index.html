About

One day we had a bunch of test,almost for each subject. When we were studying we got bored and we wanted to do something fun. So then we thought about the idea to make a trivia board game where it helps you learn your subjects in a fun enjoyable way. This way, if we needed to study or we wanted to have fun, we could just play this in our free time.

This product was made by Arman Johal, Sarfaraz Khan, and Rohin Abhol
